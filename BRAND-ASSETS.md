# Blessings Global Outreach — Logo & Brand Assets

## Concept
- **Central shape:** Silhouette map of Africa
- **Below:** Open, cupped hands cradling the continent (blessing + outreach)
- **Above:** Subtle dove or light rays (Holy Spirit, spreading the gospel)
- **Style:** Clean, minimal vector; premium non-profit / global ministry; scalable from favicon to banner

## Typography (wordmark)

- **“Blessings”** — Bold, modern serif (e.g. **Playfair Display** — already `font-display` in project)
- **“Global Outreach”** — Lighter weight, wider letter-spacing, smaller size
- **Tagline:** *Blessing Nations. Spreading Christ's Love.* — smaller, refined
- **Light version:** Gold “Blessings”, dark gray “Global Outreach”
- **Dark version:** Gold “Blessings”, white “Global Outreach”

---

## Usage

- **Favicon / app icon:** Use `bgo-icon-dark-badge.png` (or crop to square) for dark UI; use light badge on light pages.
- **Navbar:** Prefer horizontal dark or light logo depending on header background.
- **Print / merch:** Use stacked or horizontal with appropriate background version; keep clear space around the mark.
- **Social / OG:** Use horizontal or stacked on brand background (#0D0A07 or white) for consistency.
